WHO WE ARE

Aquila Core AI is a bespoke, end-to-end solutions provider specializing in the design and software development of custom AI-enabled consumer electronics. We combine cutting-edge artificial intelligence with user-centric hardware innovation to deliver intelligent, high-performance products tailored to each client’s vision. Beyond design, we offer comprehensive financial and project management services to support seamless mass production, ensuring efficiency, scalability, and cost control. With a trusted, fully integrated supply chain based in Taiwan, Aquila Core AI delivers precision, speed, and quality at every stage—from concept to consumer.

WHAT WE DO

Aquila Core AI specializes in ODM and OEM services for custom AI-enabled electronics, including design, AI software development, and mass production.

  • Custom Hardware Design Services
  • AI Software Development
  • ODM and OEM services
  • Mass Production Services to Scale Production.
  • Financial Project Management for Mass Production
  • Supply Chain Management and Logistics Partners for On Time Delivery

Aquila Core AI is your one-stop shop for end-to-end solutions in the exciting and fast-evolving world of AI-enabled consumer electronics. From bespoke designs and intelligent software development to financial oversight and mass production management, we handle every stage of the journey. Our integrated approach ensures seamless execution, unmatched customization, and rapid time to market—empowering you to lead in this dynamic new era of smart technology.
